You can create Combo type products, associated with several products. When you choose the "Combo", add, remove, through a dynamic selection, the items or products that make up the combo.

Combo type products

Enable the combo type product and add the products that make it up with multiple quantities.

Change the Items Quantity

In the pop-up window, you can add, change or remove the items that are part of the combo.

Pos Receipt

Sub-products will be visible in the pos receipt.
